# CONSUMER VOICE AI — COMPETITIVE MAP vs koe (2026)

## Scoring legend (koe's 4 moat axes)
Score 0–3 per axis. **(1) Turnkey** = no API keys/config for non-engineers. **(2) Voice-FIRST** = designed voice-native, not a chat box with a mic. **(3) Calibrated transparency / glass-box** = real-time disclosure of verifiable tool/source + a *calibrated* confidence signal. **(4) Always-on PC-operating secretary** = persistent residency + operates the local PC agentically.
- 0 = absent · 1 = weak/partial · 2 = present but not the core · 3 = strong, core to product.
- **THREAT** = Direct / Adjacent / Low. CONFIRMED vs INFERRED marked per product.

Headline finding up front: **Axis 3 (calibrated transparency / glass-box) scores 0 across every product surveyed.** Many show *steps* or ask *confirmation* before risky actions (Atlas, Gemini Spark, Copilot Actions, Alexa+), but **none disclose a calibrated confidence signal**. This matches koe's 2026 novelty research. The eroding moat is **voice-first + PC-operating** (now widely shipped); the intact moat is **calibrated transparency** and, partially, **turnkey residency on the desktop**.

## SYNTHESIS DATA FOR THE FOUNDER'S 3 QUESTIONS

**Q: Is voice now table-stakes?** → **YES, CONFIRMED.** In 2026 voice ships in: Copilot (Hey Copilot), Gemini (Live + Mac voice), Siri 2.0, Alexa+, Perplexity (Hey Plex), Atlas, Meta (full-duplex), plus every companion (Sesame/Grok/Character/Kindroid) and even open-source Hermes. **koe can no longer differentiate on "it talks."** Voice-FIRST (vs bolt-on) is *slightly* rarer — Alexa+, Siri, Sesame are genuinely voice-native; Copilot/Gemini/Perplexity/Atlas/Hermes are **voice-as-a-mode** on a text/browser/agent core. That is a *thin* edge, not a moat.

**Q: Who actually competes with koe (turnkey + voice + operates YOUR PC + always-on)?** → The intersection of ALL of koe's axes narrows to **3 real overlaps**:
1. **Microsoft Copilot** (axes 1,2,4 — built into Windows, koe's exact surface). *Biggest threat.*
2. **Google Gemini Spark on Mac** (axes 1,2,4 — Mac file-op agent + 24/7 + voice, shipping summer 2026).
3. **Perplexity Personal Computer** (axes 1,4 + partial 2 — Mac local use now, Windows soon).
Adjacent-but-not-PC: Alexa+ (home), Siri (iOS), Atlas (browser-bounded), Hermes (config-heavy), Rabbit (hardware). Companions (Sesame/Grok/Character/Pi/Kindroid/Meta) compete for *attention/companionship*, NOT the secretary job.

**Q: Is koe's differentiation eroding?** → **PARTIALLY.**
- **ERODED:** "voice" and "operates your PC" and "always-on" are now shipped by Microsoft + Google + Perplexity on the exact desktop surface. koe is **late and small against OS incumbents** on axes 1/2/4. The "always-on PC secretary" idea is no longer novel — it's the 2026 platform default (Windows is literally "the computer you can talk to").
- **INTACT (the real moat):** **Axis 3 — calibrated transparency / glass-box scored 0/0 across ALL 14 products.** The closest anyone gets:
  - Perplexity shows **sources** (not calibrated confidence).
  - Atlas / Gemini Spark / Copilot Actions / Siri show **step narration + ask-before-risky-action** (a *control/approval* UX, which koe also has via its 3-tier gate — so even koe's safety gate is now table-stakes).
  - **Zero products disclose a CALIBRATED confidence signal** matched to real accuracy. The 2026 academic work exists (RLCR cuts calibration error up to 90%; "calibrated *frequency* presentation" beats raw probability for user reliance — directly supports koe's "3–4 band label, not raw %") but **has not shipped in any consumer voice product.** koe's novelty-research claim (0 products / 0 papers do consumer calibrated-confidence disclosure) holds as of June 2026.

**Strategic implication (raw):** koe's defensible wedge is **axis 3 (calibrated glass-box), not axes 1/2/4** — those are now contested commodity. The pitch "always-on voice PC agent" walks straight into Microsoft/Google. The pitch "the agent that tells you, calibrated, how sure it is and what it's actually doing, so a non-engineer knows when to step in" is still **uncontested**. Risk to monitor: (a) Perplexity is one feature-step from pairing its source-transparency with a confidence label; (b) the *managed* layer over open agents (GMO over Hermes) is the turnkey-ification vector; (c) OpenAI retiring Mac desktop voice (Jan 15 2026) leaves a *gap* on koe's exact Mac surface — a tactical opening.
